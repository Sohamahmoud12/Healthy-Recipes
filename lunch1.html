Recipes

Grilled Chicken and Vegetables

Marinate chicken breasts in olive oil, garlic,
and lemon juice, then grill them alongside a variety
of vegetables such as zucchini, bell peppers, and onions.

Fish Tacos

Season grilled fish with chili powder and lime juice,
then wrap it in a corn tortilla with cabbage slaw and avocado.

Quinoa Salad

Cook quinoa and mix it with diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes,
red onion, and feta cheese. Dress with a vinaigrette
made from olive oil, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard.

Roasted Vegetable Lasagna

Layer roasted vegetables like eggplant, zucchini, and bell
peppers with whole wheat lasagna noodles,
ricotta cheese, and marinara sauce.

Grilled Salmon with Broccoli

Grill salmon fillets and serve with roasted broccoli drizzled
with olive oil and sprinkled with salt and pepper.

Baked Sweet Potato

Bake sweet potatoes in the oven until tender, then
top with black beans, salsa, and avocado.

Turkey Meatballs and Spaghetti Squash

Mix ground turkey with herbs and spices, then shape into
meatballs and bake in the oven. Serve with roasted spaghetti
squash and marinara sauce.

Lentil Soup

Cook lentils with diced onion, carrot, and celery in chicken or vegetable broth. Add spinach and diced tomatoes for extra flavor.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Cut off the tops of bell peppers and remove
the seeds. Stuff with cooked brown rice, ground turkey,
and diced vegetables like mushrooms and zucchini.
Bake in the oven until tender.
