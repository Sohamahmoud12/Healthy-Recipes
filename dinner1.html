Recipes

Grilled salmon with roasted vegetables

Grill a salmon fillet and serve it with roasted vegetables like sweet potatoes,
carrots, and Brussels sprouts. Drizzle with a little
bit of olive oil and lemon juice for added flavor.

Chicken stir-fry with brown rice

Cook some chicken breast in a pan with vegetables like broccoli,
bell peppers, and mushrooms. Serve over brown rice for a
filling and healthy meal.

Baked sweet potato with black beans and avocado

Bake a sweet potato in the oven and top it with black beans,
avocado, and salsa for a vegetarian dinner option
that's packed with nutrients.

Turkey chili

Make a hearty turkey chili with lean ground turkey, kidney beans,
diced tomatoes, and chili powder. Serve with a side of
whole grain bread for dipping.

Grilled chicken and vegetable kebabs

Thread chicken breast and vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers,
and onions onto skewers and grill for a healthy and delicious meal.

Quinoa and vegetable stir-fry

Cook quinoa according to package instructions and stir-fry with
vegetables like asparagus, mushrooms, and bell peppers. Season
with soy sauce and sesame oil for added flavor.

Lentil soup with whole grain bread

Cook lentils with vegetables like carrots, celery, and
onions to make a hearty and nutritious soup. Serve with a
slice of whole grain bread for dipping.

Grilled shrimp with quinoa salad

Grill shrimp and serve with a side of quinoa
salad made with vegetables like cucumbers, tomatoes, and
bell peppers. Dress the salad with a vinaigrette
made with olive oil and lemon juice.

Baked chicken with roasted vegetables

Bake chicken breasts in the oven and serve with roasted
vegetables like cauliflower, broccoli, and carrots. Drizzle
with balsamic glaze for added flavor.
